## 4. Hinglish Sentence Pattern Library

Extracted verbatim from the Quera transcript. Script generator should **reuse these patterns**, not imitate them loosely.

### 4.1 Hooks (first sentence / section openers)

- *"Kya hua agar main aapse kahu ki..."*
- *"Aur sabse crazy baat yeh hai ki..."*
- *"Aur yeh idea itna powerful hai ki..."*
- *"Jo dekh kar aapki soch puri tarah se badal kar rakh degi."*
- *"Is sawaal ka jawab jaan ke aapka dimag sach me ghoom jaayega."*

### 4.2 Rhetorical questions (stay-hook beats)

- *"Ab sawaal yeh hai ki — ..."*
- *"Ab zara sochiye — ..."*
- *"Kya yeh sirf ek coincidence hai ya phir..."*
- *"Ab gaur kariye ki..."*
- *"Ab aap soch rahe honge ki..."*

### 4.3 Credibility intro phrases

- *"Yeh idea kisi random insaan ne nahi diya, balki diya tha..."*
- *"Iska koi naam aapne suna hoga — ..."*
- *"Isi sawaal ko Nobel Prize winner ne poocha tha saal [X] mein..."*
- *"[Scientist] jo ki [field] mein [achievement] ke liye Nobel Prize bhi jeet chuke hain, woh kehte hain ki..."*

### 4.4 Myth-to-science bridges

- *"Yeh wahi kahani hai jo hamare [religion] mein bhi bayaan ki gayi hai..."*
- *"Aur sirf [region] mein hi nahi, balki duniya bhar ki har purani sabhyata mein ek hi kahani milti hai..."*
- *"Yeh description sun kar aapko kisi aur cheez ki yaad aati hai?"* — (used just before reveal)

### 4.5 Transitions between sections

- *"Ab chaliye is baat ko thoda scientific angle se bhi samajhte hain..."*
- *"Ab yahan pe kahani aur interesting ho jaati hai kyunki..."*
- *"Ab chaliye thoda aur gehrai me jaate hain aur baat karte hain ki..."*
- *"Aur yahan pe entry hoti hai ek bahut hi controversial but interesting theory ki..."*

### 4.6 Climax / philosophical closers

- *"Yeh baat sun kar aapka dimag sach me ghoom jaayega."*
- *"Aur yeh baat chahe aap science ke angle se dekho ya spirituality ke, dono taraf se yeh equally beautiful aur equally mind-blowing hai."*
- *"Sach kya hai — yeh to time hi bataayega."*
- *"Hum brahmand ka woh hissa hain jisne khud ko samajhna shuru kiya hai."*

### 4.7 Outro CTA

- *"Agar yeh video aapko acchi lagi ho toh ise apne doston ke saath share karna..."*
- *"Comment mein batao ki aap kya sochte ho — kya bhagwan alien hai ya phir yeh sab sirf kalpana hai..."*
- *"Milte hain agli video me. Tab tak ke liye apna khayal rakhiye."*

## 5. What Our Pipeline Must Replicate

Concrete requirements mapped to pipeline stages:

## 6. What Quera Does NOT Do (anti-patterns to avoid)

- ❌ No "Hello guys" / "Subscribe for more" intro — hook goes directly
- ❌ No bracketed stage directions in narration (`[pause]`, `[dramatic]`) — AI voices read these literally
- ❌ No chat-speak Hindi — respectful, slightly elevated tone
- ❌ No single-source-of-truth claims — every fact is framed as *"some researchers claim..."*
- ❌ No excessive emojis in description — clean cinematic prose
- ❌ No mid-roll "like and subscribe" breaks — CTA only at the very end
