## 2. Title Formulas (with 5 variants each)

### Formula A — Shock Question

```
[Provocative question] | [Genre tag हिंदी]
```

**Examples:**

1. `Kya Shiv Ji ek Alien the? | हिंदी Documentary`
2. `Was God Actually an Alien? | हिंदी`
3. `Kya Mahabharat me Nuclear War hua tha? | Cosmic Docs`
4. `Kya hum Simulation me hain? — Vedanta ka jawab | हिंदी`
5. `Kya Dwarka Atlantis hai? — 9000 saal purana sach`

### Formula B — Hidden Truth

```
[Ancient subject] ka chhupa rahasya | [Time marker]
```

**Examples:**

1. `Kailash Parvat ka chhupa rahasya | 5000 saal ka sach`
2. `Ramayan ke Vimaan ka chhupa sach | Documentary`
3. `Mahabharat Brahmastra ka sach jo NASA bhi maan gaya`
4. `Pyramids ka chhupa connection Kailash Mandir se`
5. `Bhagavad Gita me chhupi hui quantum physics`

### Formula C — Science × Mythology

```
[Modern science concept] aur [Ancient concept] — [curiosity hook]
```

**Examples:**

1. `Black Hole aur Brahm-Gol — kya yeh ek hi cheez hai?`
2. `Quantum Physics aur Bhagavad Gita — Schrödinger ka secret`
3. `Simulation Theory aur Vedanta — 2000 saal ka connection`
4. `Nuclear Physics aur Brahmastra — Oppenheimer ka confession`
5. `Anti-Gravity aur Kailash Parvat — Russian scientists ka study`

### Title rules

- Max 70 characters (avoid truncation)
- First word must be the hook (noun or "Kya")
- `| हिंदी` tag at end signals Hindi content to language-based recommendations
- No clickbait emojis in title; save punch for thumbnail

### Description rules

- No emoji in first 3 lines (those appear in search preview)
